About Me

Born in Singapore and raised in Shanghai and Detroit. Currently an Electrical and Computer Engineering student at Carnegie Mellon University. Most recent experience as a Software Engineer at SquareFare, developing systems to streamline operations and support company growth.

I enjoy building things end to end. I created Final Cut 112, a Python video editor with filters, stitching, overlays, and a timeline UI. In Project Projection I prototyped a low-cost corrected projection system. With Oakland University’s cybersecurity group, I explored PX4 drone telemetry and built a small full stack frontend to visualize data. I also worked on TrueTennisVR and helped design a drill routine that was showcased at the 2023 Cincinnati Open.

Outside of coding, I love playing and coaching tennis, catching Pistons games, discovering new music, and exploring food and coffee spots in Pittsburgh. I’m driven by curiosity, collaboration, and pushing projects that challenge my limits.
